Kothapally Waterfalls:

Kothapally waterfalls have been recently discovered and turned into an absolute paradise for nature lovers. Located close to the Alluri Seethamaraju district, this astounding waterfall in Andhra Pradesh offers a spectacular view of lush greenery and clear waters falling to the rocky region. People prefer this place as it can pour nature's tranquillity into one's heart.


More Details:


Entry Fee: ₹10 per person
Timing:6:00 AM to 5:30 PM
How to Reach:Take a bus or hire a cab from Lambasingi or Araku to reach this place
Best Time to Visit:October to March
Things to Do:Sightseeing, trekking, photography, etc.
Ideal trip duration: One day
