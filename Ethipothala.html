Ethipothala Falls:

This waterfall is formed from one of the significant tributaries of the river Krishna, Chandravakna River being a major source of its current. Known for its breathtaking view and picturesque greenery, Ethipothala Falls is considered one of the highest waterfalls in Andhra Pradesh near Vijayawada.

More details:

Height of Waterfall: 70 Ft
Entry Fee: ₹20 per adults & ₹15 per child
Timing: 6: 30 AM to 9 PM
How to Reach: Board a bus from Hyderabad to Nagarjuna Sagar, and then take a taxi to reach the spot
Best Time to Visit: June to September (Monsoon)
Things to Do: Exploring Nagarjuna Sagar dam and visiting Ethipothala viewpoint, photography, etc.
Ideal trip duration: One day
