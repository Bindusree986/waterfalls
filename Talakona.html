Talakona Falls

One of the popular waterfalls, Talakona, is currently one of the most visited tourist spots near Sri Venkateswara National Park. Tourists from all over the country visit this highest waterfall in Andhra Pradesh to explore its rich flora and fauna. It is home to different species of birds and plants with medicinal value.

More details:

Height of Waterfall: 270 ft
Entry Fee: ₹3 per person (Additional charges of ₹10 per person as boating charges)
Timing: 6:00 AM to 7:30 PM
How to Reach: By bus tour to reach Chittoor to hire a taxi to reach the place
Best Time to Visit: September to December
Things to Do: Sightseeing, trekking etc
Ideal trip duration: One day
