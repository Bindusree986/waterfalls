Katiki Falls

Near the Borra Caves near the Arakku Valley, Katiki Waterfalls is named after the mythological character Kaitiki. The waterfall has its source from the Ghostani River and involves approximately 2 Km of trekking to reach the ultimate destination. Tourists and adventure enthusiasts often indulge in camping and other activities like barbecues at the base camp of this waterfall, making the place exciting.

More details

Height of Waterfall: 50 ft.
Entry Fee: Free (Additional charges of ₹250 to ₹300 per person for hiring a jeep for trekking)
Timing: 6:00 AM to 7:00 PM
How to Reach: By taking a jeep or cab from the Borra caves
Things to Do: Visit the nearest shopping mall, camp, taste authentic delicacies, etc.
Ideal trip duration: 2 to 3 hours
