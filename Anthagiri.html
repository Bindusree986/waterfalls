Ananthagiri Waterfalls

Ananthagiri Waterfalls is considered one of the astounding waterfalls in Andhra Pradesh, offering the Araku valley's serenity. Tourists visiting the famous Araku Valley often visit this waterfall for its stunning view that will rejuvenate your soul and mind. In addition, this waterfall gorges down to the steep rocks, which are extremely slippery; hence, travellers should be extra careful while visiting this place

More details:

Height of Waterfall: 100 ft.
Entry Fee: Free
Timing: Open all-day
How to Reach: Hire a cab from the city or take a 20-minute walk from Ananthagiri town to reach this place.
Best Time to Visit: October to February
Things to Do: Explore the surrounding scenic beauty, visit the nearby temples, photograph, etc.
Ideal trip duration: 1 to 2 hours
