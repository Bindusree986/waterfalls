Kailasakona Falls

As per the legendary tales, this waterfall in AP is said to have medicinal value and is considered sacred by the locals. Tourists can also visit the nearby Lord Shiva and Parvati dedicated temple and spot two other small waterfalls flowing near the shrine. Offering a spectacular view to the tourists, Kailasakona Falls provides serenity and peace to the tourists enjoying its chilly waters

More details:

Height of Waterfall: 40 ft
Entry Fee: ₹10 per person
Timing: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM
How to Reach: Hire a cab from Tirupati or board a bus or any other public transport vehicle from Tirupati to reach this place
Best Time to Visit: All year around
Things to Do: Sightseeing, pilgrimage, photography, etc.
Ideal trip duration: One day
