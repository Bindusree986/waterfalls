Majestic Waterfalls of Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh, the most populous state in South India, is also called the "Rice Granary of India". Serving a rich history of kingship and other legends, Andhra Pradesh is home to a myriad of waterfalls and dense forests with different species of animals and birds. The rich cultural habitat and numerous nature offerings make Andhra Pradesh one of India's most visited tourist destinations.


List of Popular Waterfalls in Andhra Pradesh to Visit here are the top waterfalls in Andhra Pradesh and other important details that are essential to know before exploring the place:

  • Kothapally waterfalls
  • Ethipothala waterfalls
  • Katiki waterfalls
  • Talakona waterfalls
  • Ananthagiri waterfalls
  • Kailasakona waterfalls
